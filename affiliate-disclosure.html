Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: CompareElite participates in affiliate programs. When you click links on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you. This never influences our recommendations.

How We Earn Money

CompareElite is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. We may also participate in other affiliate programs from time to time.

When you click on a product link on CompareElite and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission. The price you pay is exactly the same — affiliate commissions are paid by the retailer, not by you.

Our Commitment to Honest Reviews

Our editorial team independently researches, tests, and recommends the best products. We never recommend products simply because they offer higher commissions. Our rankings and reviews are based on:

  • Independent research and testing
  • User reviews and expert opinions
  • Price-to-value ratio
  • Long-term reliability and performance

We include affiliate links only after we've determined a product is worth recommending. If a top-rated product doesn't have an affiliate program, we still recommend it.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines, we disclose that CompareElite has financial relationships with some of the companies whose products we review. This disclosure is made pursuant to the FTC's 16 CFR Part 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising."

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how they affect our content, please feel free to reach out. Transparency is a core value at CompareElite.
