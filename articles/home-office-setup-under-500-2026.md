## Setup Guide: Assembling Your Home Office in One Afternoon

### Step 1: Assemble the Desk (20 minutes)

1. Unbox LAGKAPTEN desktop and ADILS legs
2. Flip desktop upside down on carpet or blanket (protect finish)
3. Screw four ADILS legs into pre-drilled corner holes
4. Flip desk right-side up and position against wall

**Pro Tip**: Position desk perpendicular to window (not facing or behind) to minimize screen glare while getting natural light from the side.

### Step 2: Set Up Cable Management (10 minutes)

1. Mount SIGNUM raceway under desk, centered along back edge
2. Route power strip cable to wall outlet
3. Keep power strip in cable tray for easy access

**Pro Tip**: Use velcro cable ties (included with most cables) to bundle cables in groups of 2-3 before routing through raceway.

### Step 3: Position Monitor and Laptop Stand (15 minutes)

1. Place laptop stand on desk 6-12" behind main keyboard area
2. Position monitor directly in front of your seated position
3. Monitor should be 20-28 inches from your eyes (arm's length)
4. Top of monitor screen at or slightly below eye level
5. Connect monitor to laptop via HDMI

**Pro Tip**: Sit in your chair first, close your eyes, then open them looking straight ahead—that's where the center of your monitor should be.

### Step 4: Mount Desk Lamp (5 minutes)

1. Clip ScreenBar to top center of monitor
2. Connect USB cable to laptop or USB hub
3. Adjust angle to illuminate desk surface without screen glare

### Step 5: Connect Keyboard, Mouse, Headphones (10 minutes)

1. Plug Logitech wireless receiver into laptop USB port
2. Insert batteries into keyboard and mouse
3. Power on keyboard and mouse (they auto-connect)
4. Pair Bluetooth headphones to laptop

### Step 6: Adjust Ergonomics (20 minutes)

**Chair Setup**:
- Seat height: Feet flat on floor, thighs parallel to ground
- Lumbar support: Positioned at lower back curve
- Armrests: Shoulders relaxed, elbows at 90°

**Monitor Setup**:
- Eye level: Top of screen at or slightly below eye level
- Distance: 20-28 inches (arm's length)
- Angle: Tilted 10-20° back

**Keyboard Setup**:
- Elbows at 90-100° when typing
- Wrists neutral (not bent up or down)
- Keyboard flat or slightly negative tilt

**Total Setup Time: 80 minutes (1 hour 20 minutes)**
